The Imitation Game
Our intelligence intercepted

a classified Enigma transmission. We managed to recover the plugboard

connections and the ring settings, but the exact rotor configuration,

reflector, and starting positions remain unknown.

Here is what our analysts have determined:

The machine is a standard 3-rotor Enigma M3.

The three rotors were chosen from the standard set (I, II, III, IV, V),

but we do not know which ones were used or in what order.

The reflector used is either B or C.

Ring Settings (Ringstellung): 1 1 1

Plugboard (Steckerbrett): AV BS CG DL FU

Due to the Enigma machine's technical limitations (lacking numbers and

special characters on the keyboard), the operator had to adapt our

standard flag format:

Intercepted Message:

YSTRPNGEHUBDLMQSSXYESYXBRTLIEIMHCWDZUI

Objective:

Write a script to brute-force the unknown rotor choices, reflector, and

the 3-letter initial positions. Once you successfully decrypt the

message, reverse the character mapping back to the standard hex string,

and submit the flag.
